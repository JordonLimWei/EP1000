Final Project

Here is where I will be documenting the process of making my final project. The purpose of this project is for me to integrate what I have learnt over the course of this module which shall include 3D modelling with the used of computer aided design softwares as well as parts to be fabricated with machines such as the laser cutting machine and 3D printing machine. With the time span of this final project only lasting for 3 week, I decided to create something simple yet practical and it is simply titled as the Mechanical Numpad.

What does it do?

The Mechanical Numpad is simply what is named, acts as a numberpad as well as a macro pad which have mapped keys to assist the user with work and help to make the use of CAD or editing softwares much easier and efficient. It has a total of 3 layouts and you can be toggle between them. This device can be plugged into a desktop, laptop or any device that has the USB type A port and be used on the device.

What are it's features?

Some of the features of the Mechanical Numpad include:
  • 12 Gateron KS-9 mechanical key switches with mappable functions
    • Editing functions(copy, cut, paste, select all, undo)
    • Discord funstions(mute, deafen, share your screen)
    • Snipping tool
  • 3 indicating LEDs for 3 mapped layouts
  • acrylic casing
